Offering in-depth knowledge and expertise on private data security risks, compliance obligations, and optimizing returns on privacy investments, our goal is to provide you with the information necessary to make informed decisions. Our experience working with local businesses, government agencies and Fortune 500 companies enables us to help organizations of all sizes gain competitive advantages with their private data management program.

We have assisted organizations in developing and implementing solutions to manage personally identifiable information (PII). This includes making sure proper procedures are in place to enable your marketing people to do their job, while ensuring compliance with local data protection agencies, such as the CNIL in France.

It is our belief that well trained staff creates a positive environment where people feel empowered in their mission to contribute to an organization's policy of maintaining trust by doing all they can to ensure that sensitive data is safe. We can raise your employees' awareness by evaluating the current environment and proposing measures that are aimed at making employees aware of the role they play in gaining customers trust that their private is not used in a way that was not initially intended when collected.

We reduce your security and privacy risk by:

  • Creating the foundation of your risk management program
  • Making sure appropriate measures are in place to protect your data
  • Training staff to identify risk and to respond professionally
  • Developing procedures to mitigate potential incidents
